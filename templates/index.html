Now, who is Doggo Training School?


Doggo Training School (DTS) is the premier training school for dogs
that offers a specialized training curriculum to equip the enrolled
dogs with right and useful skills that an emotional support and service
dog should acquire and practice.


But wait, there's MORE!


To better hone the dogs into becoming effective emotional support and
service furry babies, DTS is also equipped with the best people who
have great qualities of a trainer and who are excellently trained at
several prestigious schools in the field.
